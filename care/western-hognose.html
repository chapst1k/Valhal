Western Hognose

Updated: August 13, 2025

Enclosure

  • 20-gallon long tank or equivalent tub for adults
  • Secure lid to prevent escapes
  • Hides on both warm and cool sides
  • Aspen, paper, or lignocel substrate

Temperature & Lighting

  • Warm side: 88–90°F
  • Cool side: 75–78°F
  • No special UVB required, but can be provided

Diet

  • Frozen/thawed mice appropriate to snake size
  • Feed hatchlings every 5 days, adults every 7–10 days
  • Always thaw feeders fully before offering

Handling

  • Generally docile but may bluff hiss
  • Support body fully during handling
  • Limit handling to reduce stress
