Blue Tongue Skink

Updated: August 13, 2025

Enclosure

  • Minimum 40-gallon breeder tank or larger
  • Secure, well-ventilated enclosure
  • Multiple hides and climbing decor
  • Substrate: aspen, cypress mulch, or bioactive mix

Temperature & Lighting

  • Basking spot: 95–100°F
  • Cool side: 75–80°F
  • Full-spectrum UVB required for calcium metabolism

Diet

  • Omnivorous: mix of protein, vegetables, and fruit
  • 50% protein (insects, cooked egg, lean meats)
  • 40% leafy greens and vegetables
  • 10% fruit as treat

Handling

  • Calm temperament but can startle easily
  • Handle gently and support full body
  • Avoid handling during shed
